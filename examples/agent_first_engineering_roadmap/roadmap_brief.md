# Agent-first engineering roadmap brief

Goal: turn a broad engineering organization into an agent-first delivery loop without pretending that every workflow should be automated on day one.

Working shape:
- establish baseline tooling, evaluation, and security constraints first
- sequence adoption from individual developer leverage to team-wide operating habits
- keep platform work connected to concrete delivery scenarios, not abstract AI theater
- publish artifacts that help leaders, operators, and builders make the same roadmap legible

This brief is intentionally concise. The durable structure lives in `source_index.json`, `reading_map.json`, and `recipes.json`.
